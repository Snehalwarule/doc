ABOUT US

Hey there, At Nidhi Elite, we blend 15+ years of clinical expertise with innovative, sustainable products to make oral and personal health simple, effective, and eco-friendly. Founded by Dr. Nagesh Chitte, a seasoned doctor with a passion for preventive care, our brand focuses on practical solutions that deliver real results—without the hype.

Our Success Story

From Clinic Insights & approach to Global Impact Nidhi Elite was born from a simple yet passionate vision in the heart of Mumbai, India, where founder Nagesh Chitte, inspired by the humble bamboo plant's resilience and renewability, set out to revolutionize sustainable oral care amid personal and economic hardships. Starting in a modest home setup during the challenging post-pandemic years, we struggled against supply chain disruptions and limited resources to source premium bamboo-based materials, yet remained committed to handcrafting safe, effective products like toothbrushes, Hair comb, pen & Tongue cleaner—free from plastics, cruelty, and harmful chemicals. Today, through relentless innovation and expert collaborations, Nidhi Elite stands as a beacon of clean, functional design, delivering practical, promising oral care that elevates everyday routines while honoring the environment and empowering consumers with joyful, guilt-free hygiene.
